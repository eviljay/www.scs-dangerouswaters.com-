Operation "Liberian Liberation"


Platform:
P-3 Orion

Location:
04-32 N / 008-06 W

Z-Time:
0700 hours

Weather:
Clear, calm seas

Conduct photo recon mission, intelligence surveillance, ASUW, and STRIKE support…

After Rebel forces have overrun the capital city of Monrovia, remnants of the Liberian National Army have scattered. However, some commanding officers have been able to keep their forces relatively intact and have established strongholds in the southern most coastal regions of the country.

The democratic reforms being instituted by the newly elected parliament are being threatened by the lawless raids conducted by the former National Army forces. Recently, the National Army forces have kidnapped several members of the new parliament. A special operations force has been detached to rescue the parliament members.

The National Army compound consists of two groups of 2-large Quonset hut style buildings. An informant has revealed that the parliament members are being held in the buildings adjacent to the only helipad. ID the buildings near the helipad by taking a photo of the helipad. After you have completed the photo-recon, attack the other set of two buildings without a helipad. A Spec Ops force will secure the buildings near the helipad and rescue the hostages. Provide ASUW support to the Spec Ops Force as they ingress and egress the area.
