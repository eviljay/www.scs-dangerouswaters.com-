S.C.S. Dangerous Waters Demo released
(Friday, February 18th)

We have released the playable S.C.S. Dangerous Waters demo today and you can download it immediately.


In addition to the S.C.S. Dangerous Waters Demo we have also created a separate Demo Media Pack which includes various video walkthroughs and tutotorials to help you get the most from the demo.

Details on the Demo and a list of download sites can be found on the Demo page.

S.C.S. Dangerous Waters now available for Pre-Orders
(Monday, January 31st)

S.C.S. Dangerous Waters hsa recently reached "gold" status and as  promised we are now allowing Pre-Orders. The game is available in a Deluxe (with 570 page printed spiral bound manual) and Regular version (manual as a pdf file). The game ships on 2 CD's.

We expect the game to begin shipping in 2-4 weeks. More details and links to pre-order can be found on the Pre-Order Page.

Campaign area added to website
(Tuesday, January 11th)

S.C.S. Dangerous Waters ships with one complete multi-mission, multi-platform campaign called 'Russian Rebellion'. It depicts a future rebellion in the ranks of the Russian navy which brings the forces of the United States 7th Fleet, China and divided loyalty Russian forces to face off against one another in the waters of the Pacific Ocean.

Using the powerful in game Mission Editor, you can create your own exciting and complex campaigns. Be sure to check out the Campaign section for details on some of the missions the 'Russian Rebellion' campaign has to offer.

Intro Movie Released
(Thursday, December 23rd)

Just in time for Christmas, Battlefront and Sonalysts has a little early surprise for all you naval fans out there. We have just released the Dangerous Waters Into movie for your viewing pleasure. The video is in Windows Media Video format (WMV) and is just over 21 megs zipped.

The video uses all in game footage and gives you a preview of some of the platforms in action that you will be able to control when Dangerous Waters ships early in 2005.
